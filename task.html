7-Day Yoga Program Strategy

Core Insight

At Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya, many students study yoga academically. This program transforms learning into practical experience.

Problem

  • Lack of real-life exposure
  • Low participation
  • Theory-practice gap

Opportunity

Practical Learning Platform for Yoga Students

Objectives

  • Increase participation
  • Skill development
  • Build engagement

7-Day Content Plan

Day 1: Start your yoga journey-Your health is your greatest asset,one step at a time.

Day 2: Mental healing-Feeling stressed or overwhelmed? Yoga is not just exercise,it’s mental healing.

Day 3: Fitness balance-Struggling with weight gain? Yoga can help you build a balanced and sustainable lifestyle.

Day 4: Prevention-Prevention is better than cure, Yoga strengthens your body and protects your future

Day 5: Teaching experience-Yoga students are you industry-ready? Gain real teaching experience, not just theory.

Day 6: Advanced skills-Master advanced techniques and build real confidence, Step into the world of professional yoga practice.

Day 7: Transformation-From better health to real skills, 7 days of yoga can transform your life.
